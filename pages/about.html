My Story

Passionate about building meaningful software.

I'm Hansh Raj Joshi, a software developer currently pursuing my BCSiT degree at Uniglobe College, Kathmandu, Nepal. My journey in tech started with curiosity — breaking apart websites to understand how they worked — and turned into a passion for creating things from scratch.

I've built full-stack web applications including a Blood Donation Portal and an Expense Tracker, working across the entire stack from database design with MySQL to PHP backends and responsive JavaScript frontends.

Beyond code, I actively participate in workshops and training programs. I've attended multiple workshops organised by Uniglobe College and completed AI training through Skill Labs, always looking to expand my horizons.
